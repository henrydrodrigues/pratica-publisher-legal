Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-05
Application name: Pratica Social Publisher

This Privacy Policy explains what information Pratica Social Publisher (the "Application") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the Application, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information we collect

The Application is designed to collect only information needed to connect social media accounts and perform publishing actions requested by the user.

  • Account connection data: platform account identifiers (e.g., user or open ID), and OAuth tokens (access/refresh tokens).
  • Publishing metadata: publish requests, timestamps, platform responses/IDs (e.g., publish ID), and post URLs when available.
  • Technical logs: diagnostics such as error messages, request IDs, and basic runtime logs for troubleshooting.

2. How we use information

  • Authenticate you and connect your social media account(s) via official OAuth flows.
  • Upload, schedule, and publish content you choose to publish.
  • Track publishing status (e.g., success/failure) and provide logs for troubleshooting.
  • Maintain basic operational security and prevent abuse.

3. Legal basis (EEA/UK, if applicable)

Where applicable, we process personal data based on one or more of the following: (a) your consent, (b) performance of a contract (providing the service you request), and/or (c) legitimate interests such as security and fraud prevention.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal data. We share information only in the following cases:

  • With platform providers: to perform actions you request (e.g., TikTok API calls to publish content).
  • Service providers: if needed to host or operate the Application (e.g., storage, logging), under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal requirements: if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data retention

We retain account connection data (including OAuth tokens) and publishing metadata only as long as necessary to provide the publishing functionality and maintain records of publishing activity. You may request deletion of your stored connection data (see "Your choices").

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information, including stored tokens and logs. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Deletion requests: you may request deletion of stored tokens and related publishing metadata.
  • Access/rectification: where applicable, you may request access to or correction of stored personal data.

The Application is not intended for children. Do not use the Application if you are under the minimum age required by the applicable platform or local law.
